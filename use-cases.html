idboxTM: Simplifying Digital Onboarding

Project Summary:
We collaborated with Intelli-Solutions on their innovative idboxTM solution, a digital onboarding platform aimed at streamlining customer engagement, identity verification, and service activation.

  • Digital Onboarding Process: Developed the core functionality for customer engagement, identity verification, and validation.
  • Seamless Integration: Utilized "Nitro Pro" for digital signatures and employed a factory pattern for handling company-specific verification processes.
